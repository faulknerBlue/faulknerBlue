'Save Our River Campaign'

Co-Founded Nonprofit for Environmental Advocacy - 06.20.2018

Great Lakes Preservation Alliance is a small, nonprofit task force of young West Michigan natives dedicated to the preservation of our natural resources in the Great Lakes area. 'Save Our River' was the flagship campaign which aimed to educate the community about the dangers of a proposed dredging of the Grand River in Michigan. As a cofounder of this small task force, I was responsible for media production and marketing.

What I did:

- Media Producer: I contacted local experts and prominent figures for our video series as well as scheduled and prepared their interviews.

- Social Media / Branding: I designed our logos and crafted our design language for all of our media, smoothly diseminating information to the community.

- Video Editor: Compiled, catalogued, and edited content into various short and long-form videos appropriate for social media.

Delaware Mines Project

Documentary of Copper Mine Concert Series - 10.12.18

University of Michigan's Digital Music Ensemble (DME), headed by Prof. Stephen Rush, was invited to perform in a remote Copper Mine located in northern Michigan's Keweenaw Peninsula. Following the exploratory nature of this project, we also took advantage of the ambisonic-equipped facilities at Michigan to auralize the acoustic of the Mine. This allowed us to have a simulated 'mine' performanc environment with live convolution reverb back in Ann Arbor.

What I did:

- Producer: Coordinated the Documentary aspect of the concert series. I was repsonsible for determining appropriate recording equipment (low-light cameras, Ambisonic microphone, and Go-Pros)

- Video Editor: In addition to videography duties, I was the primary editor for the Documentary of the unique Concert Serie

- Assistant Sound Engineer: assisted preparing an ambisonics studio for an immersive performance, with the intention of replicating the Mine's acoustic qualities via lie convolution reverb techniques
