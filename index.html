About Me

I’m an audio engineer, artist, and University of Michigan student. I’m in my last year of a Sound Engineering B.S. with a minor in Electrical Engineering. My passion for exploration has led me down many avenues of audio, including: acoustics, sound design and composition for film and video games; recording, mixing, and live sound; audio hardware design; as well as audio programming and art installations. I am eager to learn and love working on interesting interdisciplinary projects. Thank you for visiting my site, please contact me with any questions!
